Questions

Frequently Asked

What is The Second Board? ▾
The Second Board is India's premier virtual advisory board platform connecting SMEs with a curated pool of highly experienced industry leaders including retired PSU heads, CXOs, MDs, and domain experts. Each SME composes a personalised advisory board of 3–6 experts accessible on-demand.
Who can join as an Advisor? ▾
Advisors are vetted professionals with 20+ years of experience — retired or serving PSU CMDs, GMDs, MDs, CXOs, and domain veterans. All advisors undergo a credential verification process by our admin team before being published on the platform.
How does the board selection work? ▾
After registering, SMEs can browse the full Advisor Directory, filter by pillar, industry, and experience, and add 3–6 advisors to their Board Tray. Once satisfied with the composition, they confirm the board — triggering email notifications to all selected advisors to commence engagement.
Is my business information confidential? ▾
Yes. All engagements are NDA-protected. Business documents shared in the Document Vault are only accessible to your selected board members. We comply with India's IT Act 2000 and DPDP Act 2023.
Can I change my board members later? ▾
Yes. You can request to remove or replace an advisor subject to subscription terms. Board changes are managed through your SME Dashboard under Board Management.
